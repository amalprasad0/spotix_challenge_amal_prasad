Brusted Chicken

# Ingredients Quantity
1 Salt To Taste
2 Black Pepper freshly ground
3 1 Chicken (abt 3 1/2 lbs);

Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Season the chicken all over with salt and pepper and place in a roasting pan. Roast for 45 minutes, or until the juices run clear when the thigh is pricked with a skewer. Transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let stand for 15 minutes. This recipe yields 4 servings. Pizza

# Ingredients Ingredients
1 Salt ounces marinara sauce ; store-bought or homemade
2 Black Pepper 1/4 cup honey
3 fresh basil leaves ; cut into ribbons or torn tablespoon hot sauce

Directions: 1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F with top rack positioned in the upper quadrant of the oven.
2. Stretch store-bought pizza dough into a flattened 12-inch round, oiling as needed to
3. Place a 12-inch dry cast-iron skillet on the stove over medium-high heat.
4. When the pan is screaming hot, but not smoking, sprinkle the bottom with cornmeal, if using.
5. Working carefully and quickly, flip the flattened dough into the skillet and, with delicate fingers, stretch the dough evenly across the bottom and slightly up the sides of the hot skillet.
6. Brush or drizzle the dough with extra-virgin olive oil (optional) and season to taste with salt and a few grinds of fresh black pepper. If desired, rub a single clove of garlic over the surface of the dough for additional flavor.
7. Spoon the marinara over the crust -- spreading it and swirling it out from edge to edge.
8. Bake for 10-15 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
9. Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle fresh basil on top and drizzle with hot honey (optional) to serve.
Hot Honey Drizzle: In a small saucepan, combine honey, hot sauce, and balsamic vinegar on low heat for 5 minutes. Drizzle over warm pizza.

Shavarma

# Ingredients Quantity
1 Salt To Taste
2 Nan 1
3 1 Chicken (abt 3 1/2 lbs);

Grab one large mixing bowl and mix together the shawarma seasoning (listed above) with ¼ cup olive oil, ¼ cup white wine vinegar and zest and juice of one lemon. This makes your marinade for the meat. Thinly slice your meat against the grain. The pieces of meat need to be a good bite size (3-inches or so) but more importantly, they need to be fairly thin for the best texture. Put the sliced meat in the big bowl the marinade. Season well with a big dash of kosher salt and black pepper, then add fresh garlic and onion slices. Using a pair of tongs, give everything a good toss to make sure the meat is well-coated. At this point, you can let it marinate at room temperature for a few minutes (20 to 30 or so), or cover and refrigerate for up to 2 hours. Heat the pan over high heat, and using your tongs, add the meat in. Spread the meat out so that as much of it is touching the hot surface as possible (and if you need to, do this in batches). Cook for 8 to 15 minutes, flipping and tossing occasionally

Burger

# Ingredients Ingredients
1/2 teaspoon pepper 1 pound lean ground beef 1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon pepper 1 pound bulk pork sausage 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped onion salt tomato

Cook bacon until crisp. Remove bacon and discard all but 2 tablespoons drippings. Saute onion in drippings until tender. Crumble bacon then add with mushrooms to skillet and set aside. Meanwhile, combine beef, pork, cheese, pepper, garlic powder and steak sauce in a large bowl. Shape into 16 patties. Divide bacon mixture and place over eight of the patties. Place remaining patties on top and press edges tightly to seal. Grill over medium coals until well-done (pork sausage in burgers requires thorough cooking). Serve on buns; with lettuce if desired
